Wheelchair Rugby tournament

On the weekend of June 2-3, 2012, from Tampa, Jacksonville, West Palm Beach and host team Orange County converged on the Silver Star Recreation center in Orlando for the Orange County Rumble. The sport these athletes were playing is Wheelchair Rugby (a.k.a Murderball) is a full contact played in specialized wheelchairs.

The Rules of the Game

A few of the rules of the game of wheelchair rugby are as follows:

  1. The game consists of four 8 minute periods with a 5 minute half-time.
  2. The game is played on a basketball court, with goals that are 8 meters wide at opposite ends of the court.
  3. The player that is in control of the ball must dribble or pass every 10 seconds.
  4. To score a player must cross the goal line while in full control of the ball with at least two wheels.
  5. full contact with wheelchairs is allowed.
