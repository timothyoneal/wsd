Welcome to the site

life with a disability can be challenging for both the person that has the disability and the family of the person. The purpose I have in creating the content for this site is to be an information source for people that are newly injured, there family members, or possibly someone that has had a life long disability like myself. This site will hopefully provide information on something you are interested in, if you are interested or in involved in something that is not covered on this site please contact me about including the the information on this site. The advice that I will provide will be based off of my experience I cannot tell you what would work for you, that decision rests only with you.

My story is that I have had a disability all my life. I have played some type of wheelchair sport ever since the age of 5. I have not let the condition I have define who I am. I competed on high school teams like any teen that participates in sports would do. It was also at this time that I thought my athletic career would end. However, in my junior year I found out about the National Wheelchair Basketball Association this is a league of wheelchair basketball teams from across the country. Competing in this league I have been to national tournaments and been coached by 2 hall of fame players. I have also worked for a professional sports Mixed Martial Arts promotion which allowed me to travel and do things I never thought I would have done. in the past few years I have started playing wheelchair rugby.
